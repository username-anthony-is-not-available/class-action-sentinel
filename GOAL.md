# Project Goals: class-action-sentinel

## Vision

To establish `class-action-sentinel` as a robust and reliable system that guides
autonomous work and enables the generation of goal-aligned proposals.

## Objectives

- **Guide Autonomous Work**: Provide a clear framework and vision to enable AI
  agents and contributors to work independently towards the project's success.
- **Goal Alignment**: Ensure that all proposals and developments are aligned
  with the core mission of the project.
- **Maintain Clear Documentation**: Keep the project's goals and vision updated
  to reflect its evolution and to provide constant guidance to all stakeholders.
